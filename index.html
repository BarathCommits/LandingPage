The Cost Barrier

Patent registration costs range from 2,000 to 2 million Euros. This excludes 99% of inventors who can't afford these astronomical fees.

Impact:

  • Individual inventors are priced out
  • Small businesses can't protect innovations
  • Only corporations with deep pockets benefit

The Access Problem

Out of 8 billion people, less than 5% understand patents, and less than 1% actually benefit from the system.

Impact:

  • 99% of innovations go unprotected
  • Brilliant ideas are lost forever
  • Global innovation is severely limited

Fragmented Ownership

Patent rights are scattered across jurisdictions, making it impossible to track usage and enforce royalties effectively.

Impact:

  • Lost revenue opportunities
  • Complex legal disputes
  • Inefficient patent management

Years of Delays

The patent approval process takes years, during which time innovations become obsolete and opportunities are lost.

Impact:

  • Innovations become outdated
  • Market opportunities disappear
  • Inventors lose motivation

The Result: Innovation is Stifled

These barriers prevent the world's brightest minds from protecting and monetizing their innovations. We're losing countless breakthroughs that could solve humanity's biggest challenges.
