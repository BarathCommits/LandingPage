Innovation is Stifled

Out of 8 billion people, less than 1% benefit from patenting their ideas. Here's why the current system fails everyone:

Exorbitant Costs

Patent costs range from €2K-€2M, excluding 99% of inventors who can't afford these fees.

Limited Access

Less than 1% of people benefit from patents due to cost, complexity, and lack of understanding.

Years of Delays

Patent approval takes years, during which innovations become obsolete and opportunities are lost.

Security Risks

Research and ideas are exposed to theft, unauthorized use, and unclear IP ownership.

Licensing Inefficiency

Manual licensing processes are slow, expensive, and prone to errors, delaying revenue generation.

Limited Global Impact

Innovations are restricted by geographic boundaries, limiting their global reach and impact.
